About Us

We are the GNU/Linux users' group at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Chapaign. We are a group of people passionate about free software and making things.

Join our mailing list here.

You can find our irc at irc://irc.freenode.net/#gnulug or our Matrix at https://matrix.to/#/#gnulug:matrix.org

Check out our Github organization for some of our projects.
